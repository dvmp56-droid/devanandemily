Where to Stay

Kelowna and West Kelowna have plenty of wonderful options. Here are a few of our suggestions for the weekend of September 11–13.

Delta Grand Okanagan Resort

From ~$300/night

Steps from the reception venue on the waterfront. Full resort with pools, spa, and lake views. The splurge-worthy option.

1310 Water St, Kelowna

Royal Anne Hotel

From ~$160/night

Downtown Kelowna, a 5-minute walk to the reception. Great value with a central location for exploring the city.

348 Bernard Ave, Kelowna

The Cove Lakeside Resort

From ~$220/night

Lakefront resort in West Kelowna with pools, beach access, and full suites. Closest to the ceremony venue.

4205 Gellatly Rd, West Kelowna

Airbnb / VRBO

Varies

Plenty of vacation rentals in West Kelowna and Kelowna. Search for places near Boucherie Road for proximity to the wedding party.

September is peak season in the Okanagan — we recommend booking early!

Getting There

By Air

Fly into Kelowna International Airport (YLW), which receives direct flights from Vancouver and Calgary. The airport is about 20-30 minutes from downtown Kelowna and the reception venue.

By Car

From Vancouver: ~4 hours via the Coquihalla Highway (Hwy 5) to Hwy 97C. A beautiful drive through the mountains.

From Calgary: ~6 hours via the Trans-Canada (Hwy 1) through the Rockies.
